ROHIT SHARMA

Time Line of ROHIT SHARMA

  • 1987-Born in Nagpur,India.
  • 2006-Makes debut for india U-19 team during U-19 cricket world cup.
  • 2007-Makes debut for indian international ODI team (ODI Lasts upto 8hrs and each team gets 50 overs) against Ireland.
  • 2007-Makes debut for indian international T20I team (T20I Lasts upto 4hrs and each team gets 20 overs) against England.
  • 2011-Gets a 2 million USD/year contract from IPL franchise Mumbai indians.
  • 2013-Makes debut for indian international TEST team (TEST Played over 5 days and each team gets 2 innings and unlimited overs. 1 day in test lasts for 6hrs) against West indies.In the same he went on to score his 1st double century (209) against australian ODI team.
  • 2014-Makes a record of highest individual score 264 in ODI's during a match against Sri lankan ODI team.
  • 2017-Makes his 3rd double century (208 not out) in ODI's against srilanka
  • 2019-He became the first batsman to score five centuries in the same World Cup tournament. Also becomes vice-captain of indian team.
  • 2021-He became captain of indian t20 team

    • Awards and Achievements

  • Rohit Sharma was honoured with the ‘Arjuna Award’ in 2015.
  • 2015 – GQ award for Sportsman of the Year.
  • Name included in ICC ODI Team of the Year: 2014, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019.
  • ICC ODI Player of the Year – 2019
  • Rohit Sharma was awarded the highest sports honor of the country, the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award in 2020.

LIFE

Rohit Sharma married Ritika Sajdeh in December 2015. They have a daughter, born in December 2018.Sharma is noted for his philanthropic activities and promotes various causes such as animal welfare, health, and children. He is particularly vocal about the protection of animals and has supported various initiatives and organisations to promote the cause. Sharma has been sponsored by several brands including the Swiss watchmaker Hublot and CEAT. In his career spanning 11 years, Sharma has endorsed many other brands including Maggi, Fair and Lovely, Lay's, Nissan, energy drink Relentless, Nasivion nasal spray, Aristocrat by VIP Industries, Adidas and Oppo mobiles
